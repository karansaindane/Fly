Our Mission

FLY Foundation is dedicated to empowering underprivileged children and communities through education, menstrual hygiene awareness, child safety, and environmental sustainability. We strive to create a world where every child learns, every girl feels safe, and every individual has the opportunity to thrive. With compassion and commitment, we turn challenges into change. One step, one life, and one dream at a time.

About Us

FLY Foundation is more than an NGO. It’s a movement of hope. We empower underprivileged children through education, menstrual hygiene awareness, child safety, and environmental initiatives. With love and resilience, we stand by those in need, creating a brighter, safer future. FLY Foundation – Fly to nurture the future!

Divya Yadav

Founder & President

Divya Yadav, the heart behind FLY Foundation, hails from Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. She holds a BA in Economics (Honours) from the Institute for Excellence in Higher Education (IEHE), Bhopal, and a Master’s in Sociology from Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU). In 2020, during the challenges of COVID-19, she founded FLY Foundation as a tribute to her late mother. What began as a small initiative has grown into a beacon of hope, empowering underprivileged children and communities through education, awareness, and compassion. Her journey is a testament to resilience, love, and the belief that even one step can create lasting change.

Intern with Impact – Be the Change - Our Internship Programme.

FLY Foundation offers a rewarding internship program for college students and young changemakers passionate about social work. Gain hands-on experience in education, child welfare, and community development while making a real difference.


This internship not only enhances your skills but also opens doors to career opportunities in NGOs, government projects, CSR initiatives, and international organizations. Employers value real-world social work experience, making this a stepping stone for a fulfilling career.


College students are welcome to apply. Learn, grow, and build a brighter future with us.
